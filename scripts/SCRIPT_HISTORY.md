## Visual Style Guidelines (NEW - Based on Viral Success)

### POV-CYCLING (Proven Best Performer)
- Bike handlebars visible in frame
- Constant motion throughout
- "Ride along" feeling → 15.08s avg watch time

### WALKING
- First-person walking perspective
- Good for indoor or narrow spaces
- Shows detail better than cycling

### STATIONARY
- Fixed camera position
- Used for app demos or detailed item shots
- Should be brief, max 5s

### MIXED
- Combination of styles in one video
- Cycling + stationary item reveals
- Requires more editing
