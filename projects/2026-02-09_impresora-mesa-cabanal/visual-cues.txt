# Date: 2026-02-09
# Duration: ~57s

---

## Visual Style
Style: POV-CYCLING
Reason: Proven best performer (15.08s avg watch time). Continuous motion through Cabanal streets builds the journey narrative of the script.

---

## Lighting
Target: GOLDEN-HOUR
Filming Time: 15:00-17:00
Backup Plan: OVERCAST works well for Cabanal's narrow streets

---

## Item Category
Primary: MIXED (Appliance + Furniture)
Share Potential: MEDIUM
Key Visual Features: Printer is tech/unusual street find; old table has rustic renovation potential. Contrast between the two creates visual interest.

---

## Pattern Interrupt (CRITICAL - addresses 0:02 dropoff)

| Timestamp | Interrupt Type | Implementation |
|-----------|----------------|----------------|
| 1.5-2s | Quick zoom + sound | 1.15x zoom on "encontrando algo" + subtle whoosh. Items already visible in frame but not focused. |

**Recommended**: Since items are visible from the start but not mentioned, use a brief 0.3s flash-zoom toward items at 1.5s to create subconscious curiosity before the narrator redirects attention to "what happens next."

---

## Timestamp Visual Cues

### HOOK (0-3s) "Cada vez que cojo la bici... acabo encontrando algo."
- POV cycling through Cabanal street
- Bike handlebars visible
- Items (printer + table) visible ahead on sidewalk but not center frame
- Camera moves past or toward them casually
- Pattern interrupt zoom at 1.5s

### PROOF TEASE (3-8s) "Y lo que mas os va a sorprender... no es lo que encuentro. Es lo que le pasa despues."
- Continue cycling, slow approach toward items
- Do NOT cut to items yet - maintain the "journey" feel
- Tension builds because viewer sees items but narrator talks about something else
- Optional: subtle text overlay "lo que le pasa despues..." for emphasis

### DISCOVERY (8-16s) "Hoy, en el Cabanal... una impresora. Y al lado, una mesa antigua..."
- Stop bike / slow down near items
- Cut to close-up of printer (2-3s)
- Cut to close-up of table showing wood grain/potential (2-3s)
- Show condition details: printer buttons, table surface for renovation potential
- Brief handheld walking shots around items

### PROBLEM / EDUCATIONAL (16-30s) "Ahora, el dato... Se degrada. En horas."
- This is the KEY educational section
- Show examples of street degradation if available (sun-bleached items, rain damage)
- If no B-roll: stay on items, show any wear/exposure signs
- Consider split-screen or overlay: "En horas" text appears
- Return to cycling POV briefly to show OTHER abandoned items on street (reinforces the point)
- "alguien pasa y le saca lo que puede revender" - show scattered/stripped items if visible

### CONTRAST / IMPORTANCE (30-37s) "Pero si eso mismo se comparte antes... llega bien. Llega entero."
- Visual shift: brighter/warmer grade to match hopeful tone
- Can show clean items in Givore app interface (screenshot overlay)
- Quick before/after feel: street item vs. item in-app looking clean

### SOLUTION (37-45s) "En Givore, subo las fotos, pongo la ubicacion..."
- Show real Givore UI - actual upload process
- Screen recording or phone-in-hand shot
- Keep it real - actual photos of the printer and table being uploaded
- "ya estan visibles para cualquiera cerca" - show the published listing

### CTA (45-57s) "Esa impresora y esa mesa llevan publicadas diez minutos..."
- Return to cycling POV through Cabanal
- Scenic street shot - golden hour light on Cabanal architecture
- "Cuando la probeis, vais a ver la calle de otra forma" - slow cycling, peaceful ending
- Final shot: wide POV cycling away, Givore logo/name overlay
- End on motion, not static

---

## POV Cycling Notes
- Bike handlebars visible: yes (throughout cycling segments)
- Motion maintained: cycling at start, brief stop for items (8-16s), cycling again at end
- Discovery moment: 8s mark when narrator finally names items viewer has already seen
- The gap between SEEING items (0s) and HEARING about them (8s) creates curiosity

---

## Caption Styling
- Font: Sans-serif italic (Givore green)
- Color: #00AA00 or similar green
- Outline: 2-3px black
- Position: Center, above bottom 20%
- Sync: Match speech timing exactly
- EMPHASIS: Bold/larger for "En horas", "Llega bien. Llega entero."

---

## App Integration
- Show real Givore UI at: 37-45s (solution section)
- Camera buttons visible: yes, during upload
- "In-the-moment" feel: Film actual upload of printer and table photos
- DO NOT stage fake demos

---

## Editor Notes
- The educational section (16-30s) is the heart of this video. Spend time making degradation visual and compelling.
- The contrast at 30s should feel like a "shift" - consider color grade change or brief transition effect.
- Items visible from second 0 but not narrated until second 8 = intentional. Don't hide them.
- This script is more educational than usual. Pacing should be slightly slower/more deliberate than action-packed videos.
- Consider adding a subtle "ticking clock" sound effect during "Se degrada. En horas." for urgency.
