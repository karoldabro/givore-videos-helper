## Timestamp Visual Cues

### HOOK (0-3s)
- POV cycling in rain, water drops on camera/handlebars
- Wet streets of Valencia visible
- Atmosphere: grey, moody, contrast with usual golden-hour videos

### BUILD TO REVEAL (3-10s)
- Continue cycling in rain
- "Me pilla el agua pedaleando" - show wet road, rain
- "Pero sigo" - brief determination, keep pedaling
- At ~9s, slow down as nevera comes into view ahead

### DISCOVERY (10-16s)
- Approach nevera on foot/slow cycling
- Show full fridge first - looks intact from outside
- KEY MOMENT: Close-up revealing empty motor cavity
- "le habian arrancado el motor" - zoom into stripped back of fridge

### PROBLEM (16-25s)
- Wide shot: nevera alone on wet sidewalk, rain falling on it
- Cut to detail: where motor was ripped out
- Show the rain hitting the fridge (visual metaphor for abandonment)
- Keep mood contemplative, not dramatic

### RE-HOOK + SOLUTION (25-35s)
- "Sabeis como se evita esto?" - brief pause, face to camera or POV shift
- Transition to app screen: show Givore UI briefly
- "Lo subes en un momento" - quick app interaction (real, not staged)
- Keep it under 5 seconds of app screen time

### PAYOFF + CTA (35-46s)
- Return to wide shot of nevera in rain
- "chatarra bajo la lluvia" - final lingering shot of abandoned fridge
- End with cycling away in rain, moody closing
- No scenic sunshine ending - rain IS the aesthetic this time
- "Givore" - final word, possibly with subtle logo overlay
