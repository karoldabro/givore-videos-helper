# Visual Cues - Estufa y Aspiradora Benimaclet
# Date: 2026-02-06
# Duration: ~50-55 seconds

---

## Visual Style
Style: POV-CYCLING
Reason: Proven best performer (15.08s avg watch time). Cycling through Benimaclet creates "ride along" discovery feel. Items appear as mid-ride discovery.

---

## Lighting
Target: GOLDEN-HOUR
Filming Time: 15:00-17:00 (Valencia winter light)
Backup Plan: OVERCAST works well for Benimaclet streets (soft, even)

---

## Item Category
Primary: APPLIANCES
Share Potential: MEDIUM (functional value drives saves)
Key Visual Features: Contrast between damaged estufa (worn, cable issue) and clean aspiradora. The before/after contrast is the visual story.

---

| Timestamp | Interrupt Type | Implementation |
|-----------|----------------|----------------|

**Chosen:** Quick zoom on the word "calle" with subtle pop sound. Reinforces the hook question visually.

## Timestamp Visual Cues

### HOOK (0:00-3s) - "¿Sabéis cuánto aguanta un electrodoméstico en la calle?"
- POV cycling shot through Benimaclet streets
- Bike handlebars visible, constant motion
- NO items visible yet - just the ride
- Text overlay: Hook question in large bold font
- Pattern interrupt at ~2s: quick zoom + pop SFX

### PROOF TEASE (3-5s) - "Esperad a ver lo que me he encontrado hoy en Benimaclet."
- Continue cycling, maybe slight speed ramp 1.2x
- Brief text: "BENIMACLET" location tag
- Build anticipation - viewer doesn't know what's coming

### DISCOVERY (5-16s) - "Salgo con la bici... electrodomésticos... llueve, se golpean..."
- 5-8s: Continue cycling, general Benimaclet streets
- 8-10s: Start approaching find location, slow down slightly
- 10-12s: First glimpse of items on sidewalk - viewer discovers WITH you
- 12-14s: Close-up of damaged estufa - show the wear, the cut cable area
- 14-16s: Show other damaged items nearby if available
- Speed ramp: 1.3x cycling → 1.0x at discovery → 0.7x on damaged item close-up
- SFX: Subtle "ding" on first item reveal

### PROBLEM (16-22s) - "Y ahí está la pena... compartido antes..."
- Wide shot showing items on sidewalk - context of abandonment
- Maybe pan from nice Benimaclet street to items on ground (contrast)
- Keep it visual - the damage speaks for itself
- NO fast cuts here - let the scene breathe (3-5s cuts)

### RE-HOOK + SOLUTION (22-35s) - "Os enseño... subí a Givore..."
- 22-24s: Transition to action - pick up phone
- 24-26s: Show REAL Givore UI - camera interface, "Reintentar"/"Aceptar" buttons
- 26-28s: Quick capture of aspiradora in app
- 28-30s: Show publish flow (title, publish)
- 30-32s: Show aspiradora posted in feed
- 32-35s: Show notification or message - vecino coming to pick up
- App integration: REAL screenshots, authentic feel, not staged
- SFX: Subtle "click" on publish, "ding" on notification

### PAYOFF (35-42s) - "Esa es la diferencia..."
- Split visual or side-by-side concept:
  - LEFT/BEFORE: Damaged estufa on street
  - RIGHT/AFTER: Aspiradora being picked up by vecino, functioning
- Or sequential: Quick flash of damaged item → cut to happy pickup moment
- This is the emotional peak - let it land
- Speed: Normal 1.0x, let the moment breathe

### CTA (42-50s) - "¿Habéis visto algo así...? Contádmelo abajo."
- Return to cycling POV - scenic Benimaclet streets
- Golden hour light on trees/buildings
- Hold the shot - peaceful, satisfying ending
- Text overlay: "Givore" in green at bottom
- Possible text: "¿Qué habéis encontrado?" as overlay question

## POV Cycling Notes
- Bike handlebars visible: YES (throughout cycling sections)
- Motion maintained throughout: Slow down for discovery, speed up for transitions
- Discovery moment timing: ~10-12s mark - viewer "discovers" items with creator
- Speed ramps:
  - 0-10s: 1.2-1.3x (energetic ride)
  - 10-16s: 0.8-1.0x (slow for discovery)
  - 22-35s: 1.0x (steady for app demo)
  - 42-50s: 1.0x (hold scenic ending)

---

## Caption Styling
- Font: Sans-serif italic (Givore green)
- Color: #00AA00 or similar green
- Outline: 2-3px black
- Position: Center, above bottom 20%
- Sync: Match speech timing exactly
- Key emphasis words: Bold/larger for "calle", "a tiempo", "funcionando"

---

## App Integration
- Show real Givore UI at: ~24-32s (solution section)
- Camera buttons visible: YES
- "In-the-moment" feel: Show actual capture of aspiradora
- DO NOT stage fake demos
- Duration of app screen: 6-8s max, interspersed with action shots

## Editor Notes
- Key contrast: damaged vs. saved. Make this visual story clear.
- The script has a natural A/B structure (one lost, one saved) - mirror this visually
- Benimaclet has nice tree-lined streets - use for scenic cycling and ending
- Add whoosh SFX on preview section cuts (5-10s)
- Subtle rise SFX before CTA section
- Music: Warm, slightly upbeat but not too energetic. Match the reflective-to-hopeful arc.
- Color grade: Warm golden tones, +5-10 warmth, +10 saturation
