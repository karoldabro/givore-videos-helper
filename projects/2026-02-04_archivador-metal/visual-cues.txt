## Timestamp Visual Cues

### HOOK (0:00-3s)
- Walking shot, generic street/sidewalk visible
- NO item visible yet - build mystery with "casi nadie recoge"
- Pattern interrupt: quick zoom at 1.5s on a container/waste area (not the item yet)

### DISCOVERY (3-10s)
- Slow approach to the archivador
- Camera gets close: show the metal, the drawers, the condition
- Cut to drawer opening (functional proof)
- "Entero. Funcional." = close-up shots confirming each word

### PROBLEM (10-17s)
- Pull back to show the archivador next to the container
- Context shot: the street, the waste around it
- "esperando a que pase el camion" = wide shot emphasizing abandonment
- "Pasa constantemente" = optional B-roll of other waste nearby

### SOLUTION (17-28s)
- Phone screen visible: Givore app open
- Show the upload process: photo, title, publish
- "Una foto, un titulo..." = real app UI on screen
- Keep it authentic - not staged, in-the-moment capture
- Givore mention around 20-22s (~50% through)

### PAYOFF (28-35s)
- "podria haber acabado en una asociacion, en una oficina de barrio"
- Visual: the archivador in the shot, maybe a last close-up
- "En vez de eso... basura" = cut back to container context

### CTA (35-40s)
- Walking away shot or scenic ending
- "Guardad este video" = text overlay: "GUARDA" with save icon
- "Givore. Compartir antes de tirar." = logo subtle overlay
- End with clean shot of the street
