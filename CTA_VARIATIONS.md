# Givore CTA Variations

Call-to-action library organized by goal. Select CTA based on video objective, not habit.

---

## CTA Selection Decision Tree

```
What's the PRIMARY goal of this video?

├─ Build comment engagement → ENGAGEMENT CTAs
├─ Grow following → FOLLOW CTAs
├─ Drive app downloads → DOWNLOAD CTAs (subtle)
├─ Build saves/shares → SAVE/SHARE CTAs
├─ Create community feeling → COMMUNITY CTAs
└─ General awareness → AWARENESS CTAs (use sparingly)
```

---

## CTA Rotation Rules

### MANDATORY
1. **Never use same CTA in 2 consecutive videos**
2. **Alternate between CTA types** - don't do 3 engagement CTAs in a row
3. **Match CTA to video content** - don't force download CTA on engagement video

### Suggested Rotation
```
Video 1: ENGAGEMENT CTA
Video 2: COMMUNITY CTA
Video 3: FOLLOW CTA
Video 4: ENGAGEMENT CTA
Video 5: DOWNLOAD CTA (subtle)
Video 6: SAVE/SHARE CTA
(Repeat with variations)
```

---

## CTA Placement Guidelines

### Engagement CTAs
- Place at END of video (last 10 seconds)
- Can also mention mid-video if asking for specific input
- Repeat the question if video is 60s+

### Download CTAs
- NEVER at the beginning
- Best after showing app demo (natural flow)
- Can be mid-video after solution section

### Community CTAs
- Works best with scenic endings
- After showing impact/results
- Pairs well with "el día redondo" mood

### Follow CTAs
- Best for series content
- At very end, after main message
- With teaser of what's coming next

---

## Anti-Commercial Rules

### NEVER Do
- "Descargad la app ahora"
- "Link en bio"
- "No os olvidéis de descargar"
- Starting with app mention
- Ending EVERY video with download CTA
- Multiple CTAs pushing the same thing

### ALWAYS Do
- Make app feel like natural solution
- Focus on BENEFIT, not download action
- Vary CTAs between videos
- Prioritize engagement over downloads
- Let the content sell the app

## CTA Freshness Score

Before selecting a CTA, calculate its freshness score. **Minimum score of 5 required to use a CTA.** If nothing scores 5+, CREATE a new CTA variation.

| Criterion | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| CTA TYPE not used in last 5 scripts | +3 |
| CTA TYPE not used in last 10 scripts | +5 |
| Specific CTA TEXT never used before | +5 |
| Different CATEGORY from last 2 scripts | +2 |
| Trend-inspired (from STEP 0.3 research) | +3 |

**How to apply:**
1. Check rotation history for the candidate CTA type
2. Sum all applicable points
3. If score < 5, try a different CTA or create a new variation
4. Log the selected CTA's freshness score in metadata
