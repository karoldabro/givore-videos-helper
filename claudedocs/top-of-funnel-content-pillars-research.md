# Top-of-Funnel Content Pillars Research

**Date**: 2026-03-27
**Scope**: Trending TikTok/Reels content in Spain (2025-2026) for Givore channel expansion
**Methodology**: Web search across TikTok discovery pages, Metricool reports, TikTok Next 2026 report, hashtag analytics, social media studies, and industry publications

---

## Executive Summary

The Givore channel currently reaches a narrow audience of recycling/sustainability enthusiasts. This research identifies 15 content pillars that can reach a **10-50x broader audience** while maintaining loose thematic connections to the channel identity (cycling, cities, sustainability, community).

**Key strategic insight from TikTok's own 2026 Next Report**: The three macro-trends for 2026 are "Dosis de Realidad" (authentic raw content), "Descubrimientos Fuera de la Ruta" (curiosity-driven discovery), and "ROI Emocional" (emotional return on attention). All three align naturally with a cycling-through-the-city creator showing real urban life.

**TikTok Spain market**: 16.63M users aged 18+, 57% female / 43% male, average daily usage 1h07m. Ads reach 42.1% of adults. Content that performs best: humor + viral trends, hyper-realistic product content, and creator-as-ambassador authenticity.

**Core recycling content limitations**: Sustainability content on TikTok Spain generates good engagement rates (above platform average) but the audience is inherently small. The most viral sustainability videos are NOT about polar ice caps -- they are about daily life integration: "how to use leftovers better" or "why choose plants." This confirms the strategy of leading with lifestyle, not with the cause.

## Content Pillar Analysis

### 1. CYCLING CITY EXPLORATION (POV Bike Rides)

**Trending evidence**: HIGH
- #cycling: 26.5M+ posts on TikTok
- #bikelife: 9.3M+ posts
- #bikeride: massive community (51M cycling-related posts total)
- POV cycling content is an established and growing format
- E-bike content trending as a subcategory
- TikTok's "Descubrimientos Fuera de la Ruta" trend aligns perfectly

**Hook formats that work**:
- "Ride with me through [location]" -- immersive first-person
- "POV: you cycle through [neighborhood] for the first time"
- "I found this cycling through [city]..." (discovery hook)
- "The most beautiful bike route in [city]"
- Silent POV + trending audio overlay

**Video concepts**:
1. "POV: cruzando Valencia en bici en 60 segundos" -- timelapse through the entire city
2. "Ruta secreta por el Turia que nadie conoce" -- hidden path discovery
3. "Lo que ves cuando vas en bici vs en coche por Valencia" -- split-screen comparison
4. "5 cosas que solo ves si vas en bici por esta ciudad" -- listicle format
5. "Mi ruta diaria en bici -- lo que nadie ve" -- authentic daily commute

**Connection to Givore**: Direct -- this IS the channel identity. The bike is the camera platform. Every other pillar flows from this one.

**Reach potential vs core recycling**: 15-25x broader. Cycling content appeals to fitness, travel, urban lifestyle, and general city-curiosity audiences.

### 2. URBAN ARCHITECTURE / HIDDEN SPOTS

**Trending evidence**: HIGH
- Urban exploration (Urbex) is an established trend in Spain with dedicated communities
- TikTok has literally caused "visitor stampedes" forcing towns to cap access -- showing the viral power of hidden-spot content
- Barcelona, Madrid, Valencia, and Cadiz are "stars" on TikTok for city content
- Cadiz has impressive views-per-inhabitant ratios, showing smaller cities can punch above their weight

**Hook formats that work**:
- "No sabia que esto existia en [city]" (I didn't know this existed)

- "POV: descubres esto yendo en bici" (POV: you discover this while cycling)
- Before/reveal format: show ugly street, then turn corner to stunning architecture
- "3 lugares secretos en [barrio]"

**Video concepts**:
1. "El edificio más raro de Valencia -- paso cada día en bici y nunca lo había visto"

**Connection to Givore**: The bike is the discovery vehicle. Cycling at slow speed = noticing what drivers and pedestrians miss. Also connects to the "treasure hunting" narrative of finding discarded items on the street.

**Reach potential vs core recycling**: 20-30x broader. Architecture/hidden gems content appeals to travel, tourism, photography, local pride, and general curiosity audiences.

## Key Format Insights from Research

### What TikTok rewards in 2026:
- **Authenticity over production**: Raw, spontaneous videos generate 35% more engagement than edited ones
- **Curiosity loops**: Content that sparks follow-up questions drives the algorithm
- **Emotional ROI**: Videos that make viewers FEEL something (peace, surprise, nostalgia, joy)
- **Discovery narratives**: "I found this" stories perform because they match TikTok's search-engine identity
- **Slower content rising**: Counter-trend to fast cuts -- calm, contemplative content growing
- **Recommended posting frequency**: 8 publications/month minimum for brand accounts in Spain

### Universal hook structures for this channel:
1. **Discovery hook**: "Encontré esto pedaleando por [place]..."
2. **POV hook**: "POV: vas en bici por Valencia y..."
3. **Listicle hook**: "3/5/7 cosas que solo ves en bici por [place]"
4. **Comparison hook**: "[Thing] vs [thing] desde la bici"
5. **Challenge hook**: "24 horas en bici por Valencia sin parar"
6. **Silent/aesthetic hook**: No text, no voice, just visuals + trending audio
