# Givore Content Pillars — Top of Funnel Strategy

The Givore channel identity: a real person who cycles through Spanish cities, genuinely cares about waste and community, and happens to have built an app. Not all content needs to be about recycling — the channel reaches broader audiences through loosely connected content pillars.

**Channel voice**: Authentic, curious, observational. Like a friend showing you their city from a bike.

**Givore mentions in broad content**: CAN include casual CTAs and Givore mentions — but never pushy. "Si os mola, compartidlo" or "Por cierto, tengo una app que..." — NOT "Descarga Givore ahora".

---

## TIER 1 — Core Pillars (start immediately, 60% of content)

### 1. CYCLING POV
City exploration from the handlebars. Foundation of channel identity.
- **Hooks**: "POV: cruzando [barrio] en bici", "Lo que ves pedaleando por [ciudad]", "Mi ruta diaria — lo que nadie ve"
- **Formats**: Silent POV + music, narrated discovery, timelapse routes, split-screen (bike vs car)
- **Connection to Givore**: Direct — the bike IS the discovery tool
- **Trending hashtags**: #ciclismourbano #enbici #bikelife #cycling #valencia
- **Target keywords**: carril bici [CIUDAD] (Madrid 15K, Barcelona 12K, Valencia 10K, Sevilla 5.5K, Zaragoza 4.5K, Malaga 3K), rutas bici [CIUDAD] (Madrid 4.5K, Barcelona/Valencia 1.6K, Sevilla 1.1K), movilidad sostenible (9.2K)

### 2. DAILY LIFE / MORNING ROUTINES
"Day in My Life" format — most universal TikTok format in 2025-2026.
- **Hooks**: "Un día normal en bici por Valencia", "Mi rutina: bici + café + hallazgos", "Así es mi día"
- **Formats**: Vlog-style, routine documentation, before/after energy
- **Connection to Givore**: Shows the person behind the app naturally
- **Trending hashtags**: #dayinmylife #rutina #vidaenvalencia
- **Target keywords**: barrios [CIUDAD] (Madrid 10K, Barcelona 7.7K, Valencia 3.7K, Sevilla 2.5K), Valencia turismo (22K)

### 3. MINIMALISM / DECLUTTERING
Strongest conversion funnel to Givore's core message: "give it away, don't throw it".
- **Hooks**: "Cómo vacié mi casa sin tirar nada a la basura", "El truco para que tu casa respire", "Todo lo que regalé este mes"
- **Formats**: Before/after rooms, "everything I gave away" reveals, tips
- **Connection to Givore**: DIRECT — "give away before throwing away" IS the app's mission
- **Trending hashtags**: #minimalismo #declutter #ordenencasa #menosmás
- **Target keywords**: segunda mano [CIUDAD] (Madrid 250K, Barcelona 170K, Valencia 140K, Sevilla 110K), consumo responsable (57K), mudanzas [CIUDAD] (Madrid 41K, Barcelona 34K, Valencia 18K), ordenar casa (840), reciclar muebles (15K)

### 4. HIDDEN SPOTS / URBAN DISCOVERY
Discovering unknown corners of Spanish cities from a bike.
- **Hooks**: "No sabía que esto existía en [barrio]", "El rincón más bonito que nadie conoce", "POV: giras la esquina y encuentras esto"
- **Formats**: Reveal format (ugly street → beautiful corner), listicle (5 spots), drone-like angles
- **Connection to Givore**: Same discovery mindset — finding value where others walk past
- **Trending hashtags**: #lugaressecretos #rinconesde[ciudad] #urbanexploration
- **Target keywords**: barrios [CIUDAD] (Madrid 10K, Barcelona 7.7K, Valencia 3.7K, Sevilla 2.5K), Valencia turismo (22K) — use city-specific barrio names matching video location

## TIER 2 — Growth Pillars (build within 1-2 months, 25% of content)

### 5. PETS SPOTTED WHILE CYCLING
Highest raw reach potential (30-50x vs recycling). Pet content averages 12%+ engagement.
- **Hooks**: "Los perros que me encuentro cada día en bici", "Este perro me sigue cada mañana", "POV: el gato del barrio te saluda"
- **Formats**: Compilation, single encounter story, "the cats of [barrio]" series
- **Connection to Givore**: Community life, neighborhood character
- **Trending hashtags**: #perrosde[ciudad] #gatoscallejeros #pets #animales
- **Target keywords**: gatos callejeros (3K), perros Valencia (2.5K)

### 6. BARRIO GUIDES / NEIGHBORHOOD CULTURE
87 barrios in Valencia = endless content. Local pride drives shares.
- **Hooks**: "Guía de [barrio] en 60 segundos", "Lo que no sabías de Benimaclet", "Vivir en [barrio]: la verdad"
- **Formats**: Quick guide, pros/cons, "things only locals know", walking/cycling tour
- **Connection to Givore**: Neighborhood = community = sharing culture
- **Trending hashtags**: #ruzafa #benimaclet #elcarmen #barriosdevalencia
- **Target keywords**: barrios [CIUDAD] (Madrid 10K, Barcelona 7.7K, Valencia 3.7K, Sevilla 2.5K) — use city-specific barrio keywords matching video location

### 7. FURNITURE RESTORATION / BEFORE-AFTER
Satisfying transformation content. 19.9M+ views on restoration hashtags.
- **Hooks**: "Esto estaba en la calle. Mirad ahora.", "De basura a diseño en 3 horas", "Lo que nadie quiso... transformado"
- **Formats**: Timelapse restoration, side-by-side, process documentation
- **Connection to Givore**: Items found → given second life. Direct proof of concept.
- **NOTE**: Overlaps with Renueva channel — coordinate to avoid duplication
- **Trending hashtags**: #antesydespues #restauracion #upcycling #diy
- **Target keywords**: muebles vintage (150K), muebles con palets (95K), restaurar muebles (28K), pintar muebles (19K), muebles restaurados (13K), restaurar muebles antiguos (7.1K), mesa reciclada (7.4K), reciclaje creativo (14K), vender muebles usados (5.5K)

### 8. STREET FOOD / MARKETS FROM A BIKE
Food is TikTok's most universal category. Markets = community.
- **Hooks**: "Las mejores paradas del Mercado Central desde la bici", "Ruta gastro en bici por [barrio]", "Lo que como pedaleando por Valencia"
- **Formats**: Cycling to markets, tasting street food, market discoveries
- **Connection to Givore**: Community spaces, local economy, neighborhood life
- **Trending hashtags**: #streetfood #mercadocentral #comiendoenvalencia
- **Target keywords**: barrios [CIUDAD] (Madrid 10K, Barcelona 7.7K, Valencia 3.7K), rastro Madrid (63K — for Madrid market content)

## TIER 3 — Seasonal/Atmospheric (10% of content)

### 9. NIGHT CYCLING / CITY LIGHTS
Atmospheric, mood-driven content.
- **Hooks**: "Valencia de noche en bici", "Cuando la ciudad se enciende", "Ruta nocturna por [barrio]"
- **Formats**: Cinematic POV, time-lapse, chill music overlay
- **Connection to Givore**: Different perspective on the same city

### 10. CITY SOUNDS / ASMR
12%+ engagement rate for ASMR content. Unique angle.
- **Hooks**: "Los sonidos de [barrio]", "ASMR: pedaleando por Valencia", "Escuchad esto"
- **Formats**: No narration, pure ambient sound, close-up audio moments
- **Connection to Givore**: Immersive city experience

### 11. STREET ART / MURALS
Discovering art while cycling — visual and shareable.
- **Hooks**: "El mural más increíble de Valencia", "Arte que solo ves en bici", "Nuevo mural en [barrio]"
- **Formats**: Reveal, series ("murales de Valencia"), artist spotlight
- **Connection to Givore**: Urban creativity, neighborhood identity

### 12. WEATHER / SEASONS / FALLAS
Seasonal content always trends. Valencia has distinctive seasons + Fallas.
- **Hooks**: "Valencia en lluvia desde la bici", "Primer día de sol después de semanas", "Fallas en bici: el caos"
- **Formats**: Weather reaction, seasonal comparison, event coverage
- **Connection to Givore**: Seasonal decluttering, post-Fallas cleanup

## Content Mix Formula

**Weekly target**: 5-7 videos
- 3-4 from Tier 1 pillars (cycling POV, daily life, minimalism, hidden spots)
- 1-2 from Tier 2 pillars (pets, barrios, restoration, food)
- 0-1 from Tier 3-4 (seasonal/atmospheric/occasional)

**Core recycling content** (street finds, trial): 2-3 per week, separate from pillar content

**Givore mention frequency across broad content**:
- 70% no mention (pure engagement content)
- 20% casual mention ("por cierto, tengo una app para esto...")
- 10% natural integration (minimalism/decluttering content where it fits organically)
