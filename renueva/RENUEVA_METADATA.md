# Renueva — Metadata & Hashtag Strategy

## Platform Strategy
All content targets: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts

## Hashtag Tiers (Spanish Market)

### Tier 1: High Volume (100K+)
Use 1-2 per video.
- #reciclaje
- #diy
- #manualidades
- #decoracion
- #hogar

### Tier 2: Medium Volume (10K-100K)
Use 2-3 per video.
- #reciclajecreativo
- #reciclarmuebles
- #restauracionmuebles
- #upcycling
- #segundavida
- #mueblespintados
- #chalkpaint
- #diyhogar
- #decoraciondiy

### Tier 3: Niche (1K-10K)
Use 2-3 per video, matching item category.
- #restauramuebles
- #mueblesviejos
- #antesydespues
- #rescatedecalle
- #gratisfalencia (valencia typo intentional for local)
- #gratisvalencia
- #cosasgratis
- #economiacircular
- #zerowaste

### Tier 4: Branded (organic placement)
Use 1 max, and NOT in every video.
- #givore (only ~1 in 5 videos)
- #givoreapp (rarely)

## Description Templates

### TikTok (150 chars max effective)
```
[Emoji] [Item] encontrad@ en la calle → [transformation idea] [Emoji]
[2-3 hashtags Tier 2-3] [1 hashtag Tier 1]
```
Example: "Una mesita con aranazos → chalk paint + patas nuevas. De la calle a tu salon."

### Instagram Reels
```
[Hook line from script]

[1-2 sentences about the transformation idea]

[Question for engagement]

.
.
.
[8-12 hashtags mixed tiers]
```

### YouTube Shorts
```
[Descriptive title with keyword]
[1-2 sentences about transformation]
[CTA question]

#shorts #[2-3 relevant hashtags]
```

## Title Patterns
- "[Item] de la calle → [Result]"
- "Encontre [item] y mira lo que hice"
- "De basura a [beautiful result]"
- "[Item] GRATIS: antes y despues"
- "No tires esa [item]! Mira lo que puede ser"

## Givore Mention Rules in Metadata
- **Never** in title
- **Bio only**: "Encuentro cosas gratis con @givore_app"
- **Hashtag**: #givore only in ~1/5 videos, never as first hashtag
- **Description**: Only if naturally fits the narrative, e.g., "Lo vi publicado en Givore"
