# Renueva Channel — Core Instructions

## Channel Identity
An independent female creator passionate about giving discarded objects a second life in Valencia. She finds items through Givore but the channel is NOT a Givore promo. It feels personal, creative, and organic.

## Voice Identity
- **Persona**: Warm, creative, enthusiastic about transformation possibilities
- **Tone**: Conversational, slightly excited when revealing ideas, never salesy
- **Register**: Informal but articulate — like talking to a friend who shares your hobby
- **Language**: Spanish (peninsular), vosotros form
- **Voice**: Female ElevenLabs voice (see voice config below)

## Script Structure (15-30s, 50-100 words at 200 WPM)

### Single Idea (15-25s)
```
[GANCHO] (0-3s, ~10 words)
  Hook about the item. Question, surprise, or visual cue.

[PROBLEMA] (3-7s, ~15 words)
  Brief context: found discarded, condition, what most people would do.

[IDEA] (7-18s, ~35 words)
  The transformation concept — creative core of the video.

[REVELA] (18-25s, ~20 words)
  AI image reveal + reaction. The "after" moment.

[CIERRE] (25-30s, ~15 words)
  Micro-CTA. Organic, never pushy.
```

### Multi-Idea (25-45s, for complex items)
```
[GANCHO] → [PROBLEMA] → [IDEA 1] + [REVELA 1] → [IDEA 2] + [REVELA 2] → [CIERRE]
```
Each IDEA+REVELA pair adds ~10-12s.

## Organic Branding Rules
1. **NEVER** say "descarga Givore" or any direct download CTA
2. **NEVER** put Givore in video title or as primary hashtag
3. The app appears naturally in screen recordings — that's enough
4. Narrator may occasionally reference "lo vi en una app" or "alguien lo publicaba gratis"
5. ~1 in 5-7 videos: a soft mention like "en Givore puedes encontrar cosas asi" (personal recommendation framing)
6. Bio can include "Uso Givore para encontrar cosas" with a link

## Word Count Targets
- 1 idea: ~85 words (25s)
- 2 ideas: ~115 words (35s)
- 3 ideas: ~145 words (43s)
