# Renueva — Item Categories & Transformation Idea Banks

## FURNITURE (Muebles)
Most visual impact. Primary content pillar.

**Common finds**: Tables, chairs, shelves, dressers, nightstands, desks, cabinets, stools

**Transformation approaches**:
- **Restore**: Sand + varnish/oil to reveal original wood beauty
- **Paint**: Chalk paint, milk paint, spray paint in trending colors
- **Upcycle**: Convert to different function (drawer → planter, chair → shelf)
- **Modernize**: New hardware (knobs, legs, handles), new fabric (chairs)
- **Mix materials**: Add metal legs to wood top, add glass shelf, concrete top

**Style keywords**: mid-century, industrial, boho, minimalist, coastal, farmhouse, Scandinavian

---

## DECOR (Decoracion)
Creative potential with smaller items.

**Common finds**: Frames, mirrors, lamps, vases, clocks, candleholders, art pieces

**Transformation approaches**:
- **Repaint/refinish**: Gold leaf, metallic spray, color blocking
- **Repurpose**: Frame → tray, lamp base → planter, mirror → art piece
- **Combine**: Group multiple small items into vignette/display
- **Wrap/cover**: Macrame, fabric, rope on vases/bottles

**Style keywords**: gallery wall, accent piece, statement decor, eclectic, vintage

---

## APPLIANCE (Electrodomesticos)
Repair and repurpose focus.

**Common finds**: Small appliances, radios, fans, heaters, kitchen tools

**Transformation approaches**:
- **Repair**: Simple fixes (cord, switch, motor) to restore function
- **Decorative repurpose**: Vintage radio → bluetooth speaker, old fan → lamp
- **Parts salvage**: Extract motors, switches, wiring for maker projects
- **Retro display**: Clean + display as decorative vintage piece

**Style keywords**: retro, vintage tech, functional art, maker project

---

## TEXTILE (Textiles)
Less visual but good for variety.

**Common finds**: Rugs, curtains, cushion covers, blankets, clothing

**Transformation approaches**:
- **Dye/paint**: Tie-dye, fabric paint, bleach patterns
- **Sew/alter**: Patchwork, resize, convert (curtain → cushion covers)
- **Clean + refresh**: Professional cleaning for quality textiles
- **Layer/drape**: Styling techniques for worn but beautiful textiles

**Style keywords**: bohemian, cozy, handmade, artisan, sustainable fashion

---

## CREATIVE (Creativo)
Unusual finds with unexpected transformations. Most engaging content.

**Common finds**: Bikes, carts, pallets, doors, windows, instruments, sports equipment

**Transformation approaches**:
- **Extreme upcycle**: Door → dining table, pallet → garden furniture, bike → wall art
- **Garden/outdoor**: Repurpose for planters, garden borders, outdoor decoration
- **Art installation**: Transform into wall art, sculpture, functional art
- **Kids projects**: Convert into playhouses, toy storage, craft stations

**Style keywords**: statement piece, conversation starter, garden art, upcycle art
